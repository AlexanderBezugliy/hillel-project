IT and e-commerce

An endless stream of contracts, tax issues, labor relations, corporate papers - legal work distracts you from doing business. But there is not much work to do to hire an in-house lawyer.

In addition, over time, the work will become the same type and uninteresting for a full-time lawyer. And we know very well how difficult it is to find a good candidate with experience in the field of information technology in Ukraine.

Services in this area:

  1. support of investment projects for the development of port infrastructure;

  2. certification of GTS, entering information about them in mandatory databases and registers;

  3. registration of permits for the construction of port terminals;

  4. registration of permit and other mandatory documentation required for carrying out activities in the port (emission permit, conclusion on EIA, RTC, etc.);

  1. support of procedures related to the lease of state property in the port;

  2. support of procedures related to confirmation of the status of port operator, maritime agent, foreign sailing line;

  3. support of procedures related to the inclusion of the company within the port;

  4. appeals against actions related to the illegal imposition of services in the port by monopoly companies.
